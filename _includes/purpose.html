Purpose

Student Success

The Purdue CS 190 Tools course was introduced in Fall of 2013 by the CS Undergraduate Student Board as a response to the need of new CS students to learn basic tools. We are dedicated to providing students with the tools they need early in their careers and put them on a path to success in Computer Science.

Reaching Out

We are excited about providing the materials necessary to succeed in Computer Science not only to our own students but also to other students and intstitutions around the globe. All of our work and course materials are free to the public under the GNU General Public License. Feel free to fork our repository on Github and even contribute!
